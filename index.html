Our Expertise

Process Equipments

Design and fabrication of Pressure Vessels, ACF, MMF, WAC Tanks, Flash Mixers, and custom FRP/Rubber lined tanks.

Skid Mounted Systems

We deliver comprehensive RO Skids, CEDI Skids, UV/UF Skids, and Chemical Dosing Skids with full engineering and assembly.

Piping & Structure

Specialists in SS, Duplex, Carbon Steel piping, heavy structural steel framing, platforms, on-site installation, and pre-commissioning works.

Gearbox

Precision manufacturing of Worm, Helical, and Planetary Gearboxes, along with customised gear solutions and reverse engineering.

Mixers & Agitators

High-performance Agitators with Gearbox, Flocculators, Rubber/FRP Lined Agitators, and Low/High-speed stirrers.

O&M
