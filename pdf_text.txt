Company Introduction
We have well equipped own facility for Design, Manufacturing, Assembly & Testing facility spread over 30000 Sq.ft and associated 
fabrication facility in India and Singapore with each 50000 Sq.ft
Our focus is on customer ﬁrst approach providing reliable, quality & innovative product at a shorter time frame and competitive 
pricing. We are ISO 9001, ISO 45001 and Bizsafe star certified company.   
Planodrives is a company founded by a team of directors with over two decades of experience in designing and fabricating heavy 
equipment for water treatment, sewage treatment, effluent treatment, and petrochemical & chemical plants. The founders have 
worked with leading EPCM companies globally, including GE, L&T, Woods, Jacobs, Worley and Exyte 
Planodrives is an India and Singapore based company, established in 2011 to provide complete one stop solution for customized 
Gearbox, Agitators and supply of water treatment related key equipment’s.

Planodrives Pte. Ltd., is a Singapore based company, with business interest in Singapore, 
India and Malaysia.
Planodrives established by a team of experienced professionals with few decades of rich 
experience in design and fabrication of heavy equipment's related to water treatment, 
sewage treatment, effluent treatment, petrochemical & chemical plants.  
Planodrives is a complete solution provider/package integrator and our Project 
Management team has established well defined process which ensures that projects are 
delivered with in the budget and on time where still uphold the high quality of standard.
We specialize in plant operation & maintenance activity, Pre-commissioning & 
commissioning activity in  petrochemical, chemical and water treatment facilities.
Our focus is on customer ﬁrst approach providing reliable, quality & innovative product at a 
shorter time frame and competitive pricing. We are ISO 9001, ISO 45001 and Bizsafe star 
certified company.   

In India we started our operation in 2011 at Zuzuvadi, Hosur, TN. with Design, Manufacturing, 
Assembly & Testing facility spread over 25000 Sq.ft. .

cater to the niche market of customized gear drives. The assembly facility has the capability to handle 
large gearboxes weighing more than 10 tons.

drop calculation; Structural analysis, Vessel analysis (for pressure vessels; tanks etc..) with the 
complete Fabrication, Integration, Assembly, Testing & Commissioning of SKID packages.
