About Us

About us

Action Is The Key To Our Success

Established in 2011, Planodrives is a premier complete solution provider and package integrator for the water treatment, petrochemical, and manufacturing industries. We operate out of Singapore, India, and Malaysia, bringing global expertise directly to your projects.

Our company was founded by a team of visionary directors with over two decades of hands-on experience designing and fabricating heavy equipment. Our founders have proudly collaborated with leading EPCM companies globally, including GE, L&T, Woods, Jacobs, Worley, and Exyte. We specialize in Skid Mounted Systems, Custom Gearboxes, Mixers & Agitators, and comprehensive O&M Services.
